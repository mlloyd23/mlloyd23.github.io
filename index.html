Using genomic tools to understand the evolutionary
responses of hosts and diseases to the rapidly changing environment

As a member of the Pespeni lab, I'm interested in understanding how species populations will persist through future anthropogenic environmental changes. Specifically, I want to understand how host/disease dynamics will evolve through these changes. The system we use to explore our questions are echinoderms and their microbiome and virome in the rocky intertidal.
